As a UI/UX designer with technical expertise in front-end web development, I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Rajadhani Institute of Engineering and Technology Trivandrum, and a specialization in UX through Google via Coursera.

My passion for UX extends beyond just designing digital products; I also have a strong interest in user research. I love working with people from different backgrounds to understand their needs and behaviors, and then use those insights to create intuitive and user-friendly designs.

In addition to my design skills, I have taken on various leadership roles, including guiding my peers and leading campus communities. I have completed several personal projects in web development and design, and have volunteered for FOSS HACK 2021 as a design volunteer. I was also part of ISP 23 of Internshala, led the TinkerHub campus chapter on my campus, and am currently working with the design team of TH RIET on a voluntary basis, as well as leading GDSC RIET.

I have interned at STARVED as a UX/UI designer where I contributed to user research, competitive analysis, wireframing, and prototyping. This experience further honed my skills and provided me with a deeper understanding of the UX design process.
